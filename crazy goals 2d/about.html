About Crazy Goals 2D

Crazy Goals 2D is a fast-paced 2d football-style game made using Scratch and turned into a website.

Control your player, score goals, and try to beat your high score

Controls for player 1 are W,A,S,D.

Controls for player 2 are up arrow ,right arrow,left arrow and down arrow.

1v1 your friends on the same laptop
